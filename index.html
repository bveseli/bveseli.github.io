About Me

I am a Ph.D. under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Alexander Koller (Saarland University) and Mariya Toneva (MPI-SWS). I am affiliated with the Research Training Group on Neuroexplicit Models of Language, Vision, and Action, where I work on long-context reasoning and working memory in Large Language Models (LLMs). Previously, I worked as a Research Assistant at the Max Planck Institute for Informatics, completing my master's thesis under the guidance of Prof. Dr. Gerhard Weikum and Prof. Dr. Simon Razniewski. My thesis focused on Knowledge Base Completion using LLMs.

Research Interests

  • Memory
  • Neural Networks, Transformers
  • Mechanistic Interpretability
